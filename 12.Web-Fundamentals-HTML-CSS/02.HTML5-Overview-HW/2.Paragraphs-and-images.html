How to make cupcakes

  Hey guys, today I will teach you how to bake these lovely cupcakes ALL BY YOURSELVES!
The recipe is for one large cake or about 35 small ones (like the ones on the photo below).
I hope you enjoy this recipe!

What you'll need

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2/3 cup oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 cup lemonade
  • 1/2 cup walnuts (optional)
  • 3-4 tbs cocoa powder (optional)

Preparation

First, pre-heat your oven to 200C.

Start by separating your egg whites from your egg yolks and placing them in separate bowls.

Whisk the egg yolks with a mixer for about a minute, then add 1/2 cup of sugar and all the oil. Start adding the flour (about 2 cups) and the lemonade little by little until the mixture is uniform. Leave this mixture aside for now.

Whisk the egg whites until the mixer starts to leave traces in them then add the remaining sugar. Mix them together until the egg whites can form soft peaks.

Get a large bowl and combine the two mixtures carefully. Add the remaining flour and stir by hand. Add the crushed walnuts and the cocoa powder for additional flavour.

Put the mixture in a baking pan and bake for about 20 minutes or until clean toothpick.
